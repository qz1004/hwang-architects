About Us

Crafting industrial designs since 2001

Hwang Architects is a leading arhitectural firm that specializes in providing design solutions for industrial buildings, such as factories and warehouses, which are tailored to suit the needs of both local companies and multi-national corporations. Since its inception, the firm has completed numerous industrial projects, ranging from $3 million to over $50 million, with the total project cost exceeding $500 million.

The firm was established in 2001 by Mr. Francis Hwang, who is registered with the Board of Architects, Singapore. Mr. Hwang graduated from the University of Sydney, Australia, in 1991, with a Bachelor degree in Architecture. He has been practicing architecture in Singapore since then.
